I am working in the Video, Image, and Sound Analysis Lab (VISAL), City University of Hong Kong. My superviser is Antoni B. Chan. My research interests include image classification, traffic congestion analysis, and crowd counting.

Previously, I obtained my master degree in the School of Computer Science and the Center for OPTical IMagery Analysis and Learning (OPTIMAL), and bachelor degree at the Software Engineering School in Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, Shaanxi, China.
